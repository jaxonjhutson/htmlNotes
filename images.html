Images are not on the webpage, they are lined into the page (if they're moved out of their folder they'll dissapear from your webpage)

You can adjust the height and width of images with the attributes of the same name. This is measured in pixels, which vary screen-to-screen depending on resolution, to make an adaptive image, you can set height and/or width attributes to percentages.

Images line up on the bottom left corner, if you want text to wrap around them, you can use the align attribute.
